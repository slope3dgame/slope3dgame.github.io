Overview of Over the Rainbow

What is Over the Rainbow?

Over the Rainbow is a captivating puzzle game that tests your observation skills and logical thinking. The objective is to guide a colorful ball through a maze-like landscape, collecting stars along the way, to reach the rainbow at the end. Each level presents a unique challenge with obstacles, moving platforms, and hidden pathways.

How to Play Over the Rainbow

Gameplay

The gameplay of Over the Rainbow involves the following steps:

Select a Level: Choose a level from the available options, each with increasing difficulty.

Control the Ball: Use your mouse or keyboard to navigate the ball through the maze.

Collect Stars: Gather as many stars as possible along the way.

Reach the Rainbow: Guide the ball to the rainbow at the end of the level to complete it.

Instructions for Using Mouse and Keyboard When Playing

Mouse Controls:

  • Left-click: Move the ball in the direction of the click.

Keyboard Controls:

  • Arrow keys: Move the ball in the corresponding direction.

Tips to Win Over the Rainbow

  • Explore Thoroughly: Search every nook and cranny of the level to discover hidden stars and pathways.
  • Time Your Movements: Pay attention to moving platforms and obstacles to time your movements accordingly.
  • Utilize Shortcuts: Look for shortcuts and alternate routes to reach the rainbow more efficiently.
  • Practice Regularly: The more you play, the better you'll become at navigating the mazes and solving the puzzles.
  • Don't Give Up: If you get stuck, take a break and come back later with a fresh perspective.
